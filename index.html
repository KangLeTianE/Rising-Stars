Who We Are

A place for young voices to grow.

Rising Star is a kid-led online discussion club where members practice speaking, listening, and leading conversations. In our meetings, kids share ideas, ask thoughtful questions, and build the confidence to communicate clearly with others.

Practice Speaking

Share your ideas in a friendly online space where every kid gets a chance to speak.

Lead Discussions

Club members can host meetings, ask thoughtful questions, and guide conversations.
