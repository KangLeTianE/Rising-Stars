Who We Are

A place for young voices to grow.

Rising Star is a kid’s club in which club members take turns hosting meetings. In our meetings:

  • Kids propose solutions to problems and receive feedback from each other.
  • Kids practice expressing their opinions with clarity, style, and confidence.
  • Kids learn how to host meetings.

Our Mission

Helping kids speak, listen, lead
and know the world better!

Solve Problems

Propose solutions to problems and receive friendly feedback from other meeting participants.

Sharing Ideas and Opinions

Practice expressing opinions with clarity, style, and confidence, and pitching ideas persuasively.

Host Meetings

Learn how to host meetings, prepare meetings (including finding good questions to ask), and encourage everyone to speak.
